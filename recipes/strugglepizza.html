Struggle Pizza

Description

A struggle pizza is the dish to make when you have nothing at home other than some pepperonies, cheese, tomato sauce, and some bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperonies
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put tomato sauce over the bread
  2. Put cheese on top of the sauce
  3. Put pepperonies on top of the cheese
  4. Preheat airfryer to 400
  5. Airfry for 4 minutes
